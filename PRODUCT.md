# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Product owners, engineers and AI agents reviewing delivery state in a dense operational workflow. They need to identify implementation gaps, pending decisions and unreliable status flags quickly.

## Product Purpose

CAPA keeps agent-assisted work small, governed and verifiable. Its dashboard is a read-only projection of repository manifests: success means the next gap and its evidence are visible without inventing a second source of truth.

## Brand Personality

Precise, sober and candid. The interface should feel like an engineering instrument: calm enough for sustained use, explicit about uncertainty and unwilling to turn incomplete work into decorative progress.

## Anti-references

Not a celebratory SaaS analytics page, a wall of interchangeable metric cards or a presentation that hides detail behind oversized summaries. Density must never come from illegible type, truncation or omitted evidence.

## Design Principles

- Put actionable gaps before decorative summaries.
- Preserve implementation, barrido and lifecycle as visibly separate axes.
- Increase density through structure and spacing, not smaller text.
- Keep every projection traceable to code and regenerable from the CLI.
- Prefer familiar table behavior over novel dashboard interactions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Maintain keyboard-visible focus, text contrast of at least 4.5:1 and responsive access to every table column without horizontal overflow on the document.
